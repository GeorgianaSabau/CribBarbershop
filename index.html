Crib Barbershop


A business based on passion.

One of the best places in Aarhus, where men can go to cut their hair and spend quality time,
comes from the passion of an enterprising young man.

Behind the foundation of this commercial activity there is a passion for haircuts.The owner, Iulian, has had this passion for haircuts since he was a teenager, but never thought that the day when he would open his own shop would come.
After years of hard work and sacrifices the satisfactions are great.

With a nice personality, a retro and masculine style and quality work in a short time Iulian has led Crib Barbershop to be one of the best places where a man can go to be pampered.
